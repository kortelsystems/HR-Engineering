Welcome to HR Engineering

We are a process engineering company focused on practical, robust solutions for industrial clients that need high performance and long service life.

What we do

HR Engineering supports process industries with engineered systems that are designed around real operating conditions. Our work covers wastewater treatment solutions, distillation plants, evaporation plants, and herbal extraction plants, with a focus on clean design, reliable execution, and efficient plant operation.

From design inputs and drawings to fabrication, testing, installation, and commissioning, we help clients move from requirement to running equipment with a clear, disciplined workflow.

Working process for technology

A structured workflow keeps each project coordinated, traceable, and ready for reliable operation.

1

PFD & P&ID

Process definition and engineering documentation set the technical baseline.

2

Datasheet

Equipment and utility requirements are refined for the full package scope.

3

GA Drawing

General arrangement and layout work guide fabrication and installation planning.

4

Manufacturing

Fabrication is executed under controlled quality checks and build standards.

5

FAT & Dispatch

Testing, inspection, and dispatch preparation confirm readiness before shipment.

6

Installation

On-site installation ensures the package is positioned and connected correctly.

7

Pre Commissioning

Checks and calibration prepare the system for startup and trial operation.

8

Final Commissioning

The plant is brought online and tuned for stable operating performance.

9

Final Trial

Trial runs validate output, reliability, and performance at operating conditions.

10

Plant Handover

Documentation and handover complete the delivery with clear ownership transfer.
